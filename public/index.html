About Abhiyantrikotsav

The annual technical fest of the School of Engineering,
Central University of Karnataka

Abhiyantrikotsav is the annual technical fest of the School of Engineering, Central University of Karnataka — where innovation meets creativity and knowledge meets fun. It is a vibrant platform that brings together the students of our university to celebrate the spirit of engineering and technology.

With a diverse range of competitions, workshops, hackathons, technical talks, and cultural showcases, Abhiyantrikotsav aims to foster problem-solving, critical thinking, and teamwork among participants. The fest not only encourages technical excellence but also provides a stage for budding engineers to explore new horizons, exchange ideas, and transform concepts into impactful solutions.

  • Hackathons & Coding Battles
  • Hands-on Workshops
  • Technical Talks & Presentations
  • Cultural Performances & Fun Events

Every year, Abhiyantrikotsav engages students from across the university to challenge themselves, showcase their skills, and build lasting connections. It is more than just a fest — it is a celebration of engineering, innovation, and collaboration.
